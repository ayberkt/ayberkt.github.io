Contributed wildlife studies

I often contribute my sightings to scientific studies of nature and bird migration. So far, I have contributed to two bird ringing projects through sightings of ringed birds:

  1. Common Tern ringed by the Israeli Bird Ringing Center.
    • Sighted in Büyükçekmece, Istanbul, Turkey.
    • Recovery report
  2. Cormorant ringed by NABU.
    • Sighted in Bempton Cliffs, Yorkshire, UK.
    • Recovery report

I also regularly contribute to the eBird project.
